Tufte HarpJS Boilerplate

A HaprJS Boilerplate based on Tufte CSS.

I fell in love with Tufte CSS the first time I came across it. Crisp, clean, and exceptionally readable. After cloning the Github repo and playing around with the CSS, I started the process of applying it to a blog boilerplate for HarpJS.
