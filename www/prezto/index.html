Personalized Prezto ZSH Prompt

A customized prompt for the Prezto ZSH framework.

I recently made the jump from BASH to ZSH, and settled on Prezto as my configuration framework. I worked my way through every theme I could find, but none of them did exactly what I wanted. So I started fiddling around, and cobbled together my favorite bits of functionality from the different themes I came across.

For more information, please check the repo: https://github.com/iammatthias/Personal-ZSH-Prezto-prompt
